Thoughts objective reality and imagined reality

Ever since the time of antiquity humans have existed in a sort of dual reality. This two realities are the objective reality and the imagined reality. The objective reality is one which encompasses the physical world the various species of animals and plants and the ecosystem in which they interact with each other . This particular reality is where our own human species started its journey. The objective reality had an immense power in developing the human species which will eventually end up robbing its power making it dependent in the second reality called imagined reality.
